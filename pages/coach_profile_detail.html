MARCUS THOMPSON

CrossFit L3 Trainer, Former Marine

BIOGRAPHY

Marcus brings over 15 years of elite military training experience to BSK Crossfight. As a former Marine Corps drill instructor, he understands the mental fortitude required to push past perceived limitations. His training philosophy combines military precision with scientific methodology to create warriors both inside and outside the gym.

CERTIFICATIONS & ACHIEVEMENTS

  • CrossFit Level 3 Trainer (CF-L3)
  • NASM Certified Personal Trainer
  • Military Athletic Performance Specialist
  • Functional Movement Screen Certified

TRAINING PHILOSOPHY

"Every rep is a battle, every workout is a war against your former self. I don't just build stronger bodies – I forge unbreakable minds. My mission is to help you discover the warrior within and unleash your true potential."
